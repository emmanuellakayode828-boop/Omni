The Founder

I am Emmanuella Kayode, a student in the Class of 2028 and the founder of OMNI.

I started this project because high-quality study resources are often expensive or difficult to access. OMNI is a non-profit initiative designed to provide essential academic tools to students in any location.

Our goal is to offer high-yield practice for all major subjects, including SAT and ACT preparation. We believe that every student deserves the chance to succeed, regardless of where they are studying in the world.
